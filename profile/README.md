## Who we are

Datamart is the Palo Alto engineering team that startups call when they need to **build AI products and secure them**. Most agencies do one or the other. We do both, under one roof, with the same engineers who shipped to production yesterday.

Founded in 2011. Operating from Palo Alto, California with delivery teams in Pakistan and a secondary office in Viernheim, Germany. **Over 15 years** of building software for companies ranging from Series A startups to Fortune 500 enterprises.

Our founder and CEO, **Muhammad Asif**, was named **Young Business Leader of the Year 2024** at the Forttuna Global Excellence Awards in Dubai, and is listed in the Forttuna PowerList 100.
